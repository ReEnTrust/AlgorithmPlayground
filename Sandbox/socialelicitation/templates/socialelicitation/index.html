A Social Experiment

This website is aimed at collecting the general opinion of the society about which hotels should be recommended to a fictional user with respect to his characteristics (age, gender, ideal price, etc.).

At each step, you will have to choose between two recommendations for a random user outputted by two different recommender systems.

Please note that the data and the recommender systems used in this experiment are fictional.
